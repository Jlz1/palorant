Omen

Controller

A phantom of a memory, Omen hunts in the shadows. He renders enemies blind, teleports across the field, then lets paranoia take hold.

ABILITIES

Paranoia

Send out an ethereal shadow in a straight line, blinding anyone it touches for a short duration.

Dark Cover

Conjure a shadow sphere that can be thrown to create a blocking vision cloud.

Shrouded Step

After a delay, teleport a short distance to the targeted location.

From the Shadows

Select anywhere on the map to teleport and reform. When arriving, appear as a shadow that can be destroyed by enemies.
