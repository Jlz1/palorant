Neon

Duelist

Neon electrifies the battlefield with her speed, using her abilities to overwhelm enemies and control the tempo of every fight.

ABILITIES

Relay Bolt

Throw a blast that bounces off surfaces, emitting a concussive shock that stuns enemies in its path.

High Gear

Deploy two walls of electricity that extend forward, boosting movement speed for anyone in their path.

Fast Lane

Release a pulse of electricity that electrifies enemies in a line, dealing damage and briefly stunning them.

Overdrive

Enter Overdrive, gaining a boost to movement speed and fire rate, allowing you to unleash rapid electric shots.
