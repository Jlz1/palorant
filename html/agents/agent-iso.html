Iso

Controller

Iso harnesses the power of ice, using her abilities to control the battlefield with freezing walls and slowing enemies.

ABILITIES

Undercut

Throw molecural orb that can pass through solid object.

Double Tap

Generate orb to the enemy you damage or killed.

Contigency

Assemble prismatic energy and push an indestructible wall.

Kill Contract

Pull you and enemy hit into arena to duel to the death.
